About Me

My story

Looking back to those days, the Limitations of the Internet were enormous. My first computer experience was at our family friend's home playing games. Having a computer was a dream for me.
Later in 2006, we got our hands on the first computer. It was a Pentium 4, 512MB RAM model running on Windows XP. Later, I started exploring the applications and games running on an onboard GPU. I learned about designing PowerPoint Presentations and Word Documents.
I remember being fascinated by the various fonts and graphics. I experienced challenges like driver issues, viruses, burnt SMPS, etc. The Bad User Experience was annoying until new and updated software came out. Some of the aspects were good while others became worse. I remember downloading Skins for Windows Media Player, Screen Savers, and Desktop Themes for a better experience. I became more comfortable with hardware learning the basics of things inside the Cabinet. I took Computer Elective during high school. I designed Presentations and won prizes for the same. After Completing Senior School, I applied for B.C.A degree and completed the same. I learned to assemble a computer and learned some programming.
But later, I realized that coding and programming were not my things. I learned the basics of Psychology and Design, Various tools, and Social Media Managing. I learned about a profession where technology meets design and human values intersect. UX is the ultimate world where one can make a change or influence the change which can make life better. So here I am still making a difference and contributing to the next big venture.
